About Me

These days I have business degree, but was still craving that challenge. I finally decided to take the plunge and work my hardest to become a full-stack web developer!

We are now a few months into the cirriculum, and my projects can be viewed on the portfolio link above. On the front-end side of developemnt, I have enjoyed the challengeof making a website look and feel simple but effective. Javascript has been some of my favorite code, understanding the functionality of so many different programs. I have immensely enjoyed the front-end development of websites, and look forward to learning new back-end skills to complete my full-stack exeprtise.

I am looking forward to the rest of this class, and the future beyond it. My desire is to utilize these skills in any capacity within the video game industry, where I can continue to grow and hone my skills for years to come. Thank you for stopping by!
